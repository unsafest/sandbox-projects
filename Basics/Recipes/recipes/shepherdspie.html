Shepherd's pie

Home

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds lean ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1-2 cups frozen vegetables such as peas, carrots, corn, green beans (optional)
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 pounds potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook beef and onion until meat is browned and onion is tender. Drain grease.
  3. Stir in vegetables, tomato paste, water, Worcestershire sauce, garlic, salt and pepper.
  4. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer 15 minutes.
  5. Meanwhile, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add potatoes and cook until tender but still firm, about 15 minutes. Drain and mash.
  6. Stir in butter, milk, salt and pepper.
  7. Spoon meat mixture into a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  8. Spread mashed potatoes over meat. Sprinkle with shredded Cheddar cheese.
  9. Bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes, or until golden brown.
