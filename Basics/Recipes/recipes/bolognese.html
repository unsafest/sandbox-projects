Bolognese

Home

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup red wine
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 (16 ounce) package spaghetti

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Cook onion in oil until translucent. Stir in garlic, and cook for 1 minute.
  3. Add ground beef, and cook until brown.
  4. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, and wine.
  5. Season with basil, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  6. Simmer spaghetti sauce for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
